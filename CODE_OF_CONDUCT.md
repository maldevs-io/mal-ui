# Code of Conduct

MAL UI is an open-source project for people who want to build and improve a
shared React component library together. We want this community to be welcoming,
practical, and respectful.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Be kind and constructive in issues, reviews, and discussions.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Respect different experience levels, backgrounds, time zones, and languages.
- Help keep discussions focused on the project and its users.

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic, disruptive, or bad-faith behavior.

## Reporting

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers.
For sensitive reports, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub or open a
minimal issue asking for a private contact path without including private
details.

Maintainers will review reports respectfully and as quickly as they reasonably
can. People involved in a report are expected to keep details confidential.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, including:

- A private warning.
- Editing or deleting comments.
- Temporarily limiting participation.
- Removing access to project spaces.
- Reporting serious abuse to the relevant platform.

The goal is to protect contributors and keep the project healthy.
